Project on Console

First install Visual Studios. Then open the app and install the Live Server. Next click on 'File' and open the folder you want to open. Next make the index.html file. You are ready to code.

The console screen is used to see errors on the code.(If any) To open the console screen : Right click on the screen and click inspect. A side bar will appear. Then click on console on top of the side bar. The console screen is open.
